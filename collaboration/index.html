Collaboration

Partner with Chassis. Only where the work actually fits.

This page is for professionals, businesses, consultants, studios, and service providers who may refer clients to Chassis, support Chassis projects, or collaborate on client work where structure, operations, systems, or execution infrastructure are needed.

Not networking. Useful alignment.

Not vague partnerships. Clear roles.

Not random referrals. Trusted standards.

COLLABORATION MODELS

Clear ways to work together without blurring roles.

Collaboration only makes sense when both sides create a stronger result for the client.

Chassis is not looking for loose exposure, random partnerships, or decorative collaborations. The right collaboration has a clear reason: a client needs structure, operations, execution systems, digital infrastructure, or business clarity, and each side knows exactly what it contributes.

The goal is simple: better outcomes, cleaner responsibility, stronger delivery, and relationships that can be trusted repeatedly.

01

Referral Partnership

For professionals who meet businesses that need structure, operations, systems, or execution support.

This model is for consultants, designers, marketers, developers, accountants, hospitality professionals, or service providers who often see clients struggling with unclear offers, weak systems, messy operations, or poor execution structure.

When the fit is right, the partner refers the client to Chassis. The relationship stays clean, professional, and based on trust.

02

Project Support

For specialists who can support Chassis work when a project needs a skill outside the core structure work.

Some Chassis projects may need photography, videography, design, development, legal input, accounting support, hospitality expertise, or other specialist services.

This model is not a vague partnership. It is specific project involvement with a clear scope, defined expectations, and professional delivery standards.

03

Service Exchange

For trusted professionals where both sides can support each other without forcing a formal partnership.

In some cases, Chassis may rely on a trusted professional for a specific service, while that professional may rely on Chassis when their clients need business structure, operations, websites, systems, or execution infrastructure.

This works only when standards, communication, and reliability are already strong.

04

Long-Term Trusted Partner

For relationships that prove useful, reliable, and valuable over time.

Some collaborations may become long-term trusted partnerships after repeated quality, clean communication, and aligned standards.

Chassis does not give that label quickly. It is earned through consistency, professionalism, and real results.

PARTNER FIT

This page is not for everyone. That is the point.

Chassis collaborates with people who make the work stronger, not louder.

Good fit

  • consultants, creatives, developers, or specialists with serious delivery standards
  • professionals who meet clients with structure, operations, or execution problems
  • businesses that want clean role division and clear responsibility
  • service providers who value trust, communication, and long-term relationships
  • partners who care about client outcomes, not just visibility

Not a fit

  • vague collaboration requests with no clear purpose
  • people looking for exposure without delivery value
  • unclear referrals with no client context
  • partnerships where roles, money, or expectations are blurred
  • any collaboration that weakens client trust or lowers the work standard

Clear roles protect the relationship.

Trust is built through repeated delivery.

Referrals should be clean, not casual.

Partnerships should make the client outcome stronger.

Exclusive collaboration is earned, not claimed.
