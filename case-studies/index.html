FEATURED CASE

Chef Michel Karam

A live business build, from undefined expertise to a structured consulting direction.

The starting point was not a marketing problem. It was a structure problem. There was strong experience, credibility, and potential, but the business direction was not packaged clearly enough to sell as a consulting offer.

Before Chassis, the work existed mostly as reputation and experience. There was no clear consulting model, no service architecture, no pricing logic, no defined website structure, and no clean way for potential clients to understand what Chef Michel could actually do for them.

Chassis structured the consulting direction, clarified the offer logic, shaped the positioning, built the website foundation, and created a clearer execution path for the business to present itself as a serious culinary consulting service.
