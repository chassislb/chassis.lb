From executive chef experience to a clearer culinary consulting direction.

Chef Michel had strong operational and culinary expertise, but the business direction needed to move beyond job-seeking and personal reputation. The opportunity was not just to “create a website.” It was to structure the expertise into a clearer consulting position.

Chassis worked on the consulting direction, service logic, website structure, positioning, and execution environment needed to present his work as a higher-value culinary consulting practice.

The result is a clearer professional direction, stronger service presentation, and a digital foundation that supports consulting conversations instead of simply displaying a chef profile.
