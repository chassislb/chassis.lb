The Ark Networks

Operational setup and project coordination for a business being built from pre-launch into a functioning structured environment.

This work involved building operational foundations from an early stage — including workflows, reporting logic, hiring support, supplier coordination, and internal structure.

It reflects one of the clearest examples of Chassis working close to the base of a business: helping shape how the operation functions, not just how it looks.

03

Le Pavé Bar

Turnaround work focused on debt control, operational discipline, leakage correction, and stronger daily revenue performance.

This case was rooted in operational correction inside a live venue environment — where financial leakage, weak control, and poor discipline were creating pressure on the business.

The work included restoring payment accountability, tightening operations, improving service consistency, and helping shift weekday performance from roughly $50–$100 to around $200–$250+ daily.

Scope

  • Operational turnaround
  • Debt and leakage correction
  • Workflow and service discipline
  • Revenue-focused restructuring
04

Multi-stakeholder project coordination built around deadlines, communication flow, and controlled delivery.

This case reflects Chassis in a live event environment where coordination mattered as much as execution. The work involved task allocation, follow-up, stakeholder communication, and deadline control.

It demonstrates the execution side of Chassis clearly: keeping moving parts aligned so the project can be delivered within scope and on time.

Scope

  • Project coordination
  • Task and deadline management
  • Stakeholder communication
  • Execution oversight
