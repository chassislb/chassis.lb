About Chassis

Chassis is a consulting practice founded by Sophia Ayoubi, focused on structuring businesses, designing operations, and managing projects with clarity and discipline.

Many businesses struggle not because of lack of effort, but because their work is not structured. Chassis helps founders and small teams organize execution, define responsibilities, and implement operational systems that make work predictable and scalable.

The objective is simple: turn ideas into structured execution.

Sophia Ayoubi
Founder & Consultant

"I build structure that makes execution calm, predictable, and scalable."
