About Chassis

Chassis is a consulting practice founded by Sophia Ayoubi, focused on structuring businesses, designing operations, managing projects, and building websites with clarity and discipline.

Many businesses struggle not because of lack of effort, but because their work is not structured. Chassis helps founders and small teams organize execution, define responsibilities, implement operational systems, and create brand-aligned digital presence that makes the business clearer, more predictable, and easier to scale.

The objective is simple: turn ideas into structured execution.
